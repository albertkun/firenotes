FirePad

A quick popup notepad for copying and pasting content.

Privacy

FirePad stores your notes locally in your browser profile using browser.storage.local. It does not send data to any server, does not include analytics, and only uses the clipboard when you press the Copy or Paste-as-plain-text buttons.
